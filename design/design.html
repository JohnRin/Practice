Season 1

When man-eating Titans first appeared 100 years ago, humans found safety behind massive walls that stopped the giants in their tracks. But the safety they have had for so long is threatened when a colossal Titan smashes through the barriers, causing a flood of the giants into what had been the humans' safe zone. During the carnage that follows, soldier Eren Jaeger sees one of the creatures devour his mother, which leads him to vow that he will kill every Titan. He enlists some friends who survived to help him, and that group is humanity's last hope for avoiding extinction at the hands of the monsters.

Season 2

Ever since the arrival of the Colossal Titan, the peace of humanity crumble, and Eren Yeager's life becomes an endless battle... Witnessing his mother powerlessly devoured by the Titan, Eren swore to wipe every single Titan from the world. However, as he fights, he ended up finding himself turning into a Titan――. In order to win back humanity's freedom, Eren uses his Titan power and fight the ""Female Titan"" at Stohess District of Wall Sina. The harsh battle between the Titan is won by Eren. However, the world did not let Eren nor Humanity have a single rest. The next fight already began. How will humanity fight against the sudden horde of Titan appearing in Wall Rose?
