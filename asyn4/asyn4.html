About Us

Food Wars Anime Restaurant

Welcome to Food Wars Anime Restaurant! We bring the thrilling world of "Food Wars!" anime to life through our delicious dishes. Our talented chefs recreate the unforgettable recipes that will transport you into the anime's culinary battles. Using the finest ingredients, we serve mouthwatering meals that capture the essence of the show. Join us for an immersive dining experience where food and anime meet. Enjoy exceptional flavors, friendly service, and a warm atmosphere. Come and indulge in the magic of Food Wars Anime Restaurant. Bon appétit!

Anime Food Menu

A Triple Potato Udon From Food Wars!

The Triple Potato Udon is a special udon dish created by the talented chefs of Totsuki Culinary Academy in the story. As the name suggests, it features three different types of potatoes as its main ingredients, combined with udon noodles.

Price: ₱ 350

Marui's White Potage Blanc Curry Udon

Marui's White Potage Blanc Curry Udon is another mouthwatering dish from the anime and manga series "Food Wars! Shokugeki no Soma." It is a unique fusion dish that combines elements of French cuisine with Japanese udon noodles.

Price: ₱ 300

Chaliapin Steak

The dish consists of a thick, juicy steak coated with breadcrumbs and then deep-fried or pan-fried to achieve a crispy exterior. The steak is typically served with a rich sauce, often made from demi-glace or a combination of soy sauce, butter, and Worcestershire sauce.

Price: ₱ 450

Pepper Mackerel (Spanish) with a Cabbage Purée Garnish

Pepper Mackerel with a Cabbage Purée Garnish is a delicious and flavorful dish that combines Spanish influences with a creative twist. It features the robust flavors of pepper-seasoned mackerel fillets accompanied by a smooth and creamy cabbage purée.

Price: ₱ 600

Capitone in Umido

Capitone in Umido is a traditional Italian recipe that involves stewing or braising cuttlefish (capitone) in a flavorful tomato-based sauce. The cuttlefish is typically cleaned and cut into pieces, then cooked with onions, garlic, tomatoes, white wine, and various herbs and spices such as parsley, bay leaves, and chili flakes. The dish is slow-cooked until the cuttlefish becomes tender.

Price: ₱ 650

The Mini Soufflé Omelette from "Food Wars!"

The Mini Soufflé Omelette is a delightful and visually stunning dish that was featured in the anime and manga series "Food Wars! Shokugeki no Soma." It is a unique twist on the classic omelette, incorporating the airy and fluffy texture of a soufflé.

Price: ₱ 500
